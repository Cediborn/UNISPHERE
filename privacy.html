Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

ClashBoard is a static, client-side tool. This policy is short because there isn't much to say.

What we collect

Nothing. There is no server behind this site, no account system, and no analytics script reporting your activity anywhere. The courses, times, and venues you type in never leave your device.

What we store

Your schedule is saved using your browser's local storage, a small amount of data kept on your own device so your board is still there next time you visit. It is never transmitted, synced, or visible to anyone else — including us. Clearing your browser data, using a different browser, or using private/incognito mode will remove it.

Cookies

ClashBoard does not use cookies or any third-party tracking.

Fonts

Typefaces are loaded from Google Fonts, which may see your IP address as part of serving the font files, subject to Google's own policies. No other third-party service is used.

Changes

If this policy changes, the "last updated" date above will change with it.
